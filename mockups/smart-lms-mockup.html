Definition
What is the derivative f′(x)?
Tap to reveal
Answer
The instantaneous rate of change of f at x — the limit of the average rate of change as the interval shrinks to zero.
Rule
Power rule for d/dx [xⁿ]?
Tap to reveal
Answer
n · xⁿ⁻¹. Example: d/dx[x⁴] = 4x³.
Rule
The product rule?
Tap to reveal
Answer
(fg)′ = f′g + fg′. Differentiate one factor at a time, keep the other.
Concept
What does f′(x) = 0 tell you?
Tap to reveal
Answer
A critical point — a candidate local maximum, minimum, or inflection where the tangent is horizontal.

What is the derivative of f(x) = 3x² + 5x − 7 ?
A 6x + 5x
B 6x + 5
C 3x + 5
D 6x + 5x − 7
Apply the power rule term by term: 3x²→6x, 5x→5, the constant −7 drops out.
